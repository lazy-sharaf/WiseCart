Getting Started

Learn how to use wisecart effectively

What is wisecart?

wisecart is a price comparison platform that helps you find the best deals across multiple online stores in Bangladesh. We aggregate product information, prices, and reviews to help you make informed shopping decisions.

How do I start using wisecart?

Getting started is easy! Simply:

  1. Use the search bar on the homepage to find products
  2. Browse through the search results and compare prices
  3. Click on any product to view detailed information
  4. Visit the store's website to make your purchase

Is wisecart free to use?

Yes! wisecart is completely free to use. We don't charge any fees for our price comparison services. Our revenue comes from partnerships with online stores, not from our users.

Search & Compare

Master the art of finding the best deals

How do I search for products?

Simply type the product name, brand, or category in the search bar on our homepage. For better results, try using specific terms like "Samsung Galaxy S23" instead of just "phone".

How do I compare prices?

After searching for a product, you'll see a list of results from different stores. Each result shows:

  • Product name and image
  • Price from each store
  • Store name and rating
  • Stock availability
  • Direct link to the store

How often are prices updated?

We update our prices regularly throughout the day. However, prices can change quickly, so we recommend checking the store's website for the most current price before making a purchase.

What stores do you compare?

We partner with major online stores in Bangladesh including Startech and many others. We're constantly adding new stores to provide you with more options and better deals.

Account & Profile

Manage your account and preferences

Do I need an account to use wisecart?

No, you don't need an account to search and compare prices. However, creating an account allows you to save favorite products, track price history, and receive personalized recommendations.

How do I create an account?

Click on the profile icon in the top right corner and select "Sign Up". You can create an account using your email address or social media accounts like Google or Facebook.

How do I reset my password?

If you've forgotten your password, click on "Forgot Password" on the login page. Enter your email address and we'll send you a link to reset your password.

Troubleshooting

Solutions to common issues

Search results are not showing

If you're not getting search results, try these solutions:

  • Check your internet connection
  • Try different search terms
  • Clear your browser cache and cookies
  • Try refreshing the page

Prices seem outdated

While we update prices regularly, online store prices can change frequently. Always check the store's website for the most current price before making a purchase. If you notice consistently outdated prices, please report it to us.

Website is loading slowly

If the website is loading slowly, try:

  • Check your internet connection
  • Clear your browser cache
  • Try a different browser
  • Disable browser extensions temporarily

Can't access certain features

Some features may require JavaScript to be enabled in your browser. Make sure JavaScript is enabled and try refreshing the page. If the issue persists, try using a different browser.
