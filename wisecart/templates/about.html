About wisecart

Your trusted companion in the world of online shopping. We're revolutionizing how people discover and compare prices across Bangladesh's top online stores.

Our Mission

To empower consumers with intelligent price comparison tools that save time, money, and effort. We believe everyone deserves access to the best deals without the hassle of visiting multiple websites.

Our Vision

To become Bangladesh's most trusted and comprehensive price comparison platform, helping millions of consumers make informed shopping decisions every day.

What We Do

Deal Discovery

Trusted Reviews

Access verified reviews and ratings to make informed decisions about products and stores.

Our Story

Founded in 2025, wisecart was born from a simple frustration: the overwhelming amount of time spent comparing prices across different online stores in Bangladesh.

Our team of tech enthusiasts and shopping experts came together with a shared vision - to create a platform that would eliminate the hassle of price comparison and make smart shopping accessible to everyone.

Today, we're proud to serve thousands of users across Bangladesh, helping them save time and money while discovering the best deals from trusted retailers.
